# Pzhisen Payments

- **China**: Free bank transfer → your bank card (no third-party fees)
- **International**: PayPal

No WeChat, Alipay, or XunhuPay required.

See [PAYMENTS-CN.md](./PAYMENTS-CN.md) for bank setup.
