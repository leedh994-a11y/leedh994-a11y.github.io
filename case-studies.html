Customer story

How an ecommerce support team automated 80% of its daily questions.

An illustrative case study based on the operating figures provided by a Sitp GPT customer.

The challenge

The ecommerce website received approximately 100 customer-service questions per day. Agents repeatedly handled order tracking, returns, delivery, product information, and policy questions, leaving less time for complex cases.

The approach

The team trained an AI assistant on approved website pages, product and policy FAQs, and support documentation. The assistant was embedded on the website to answer routine questions, while unclear requests were routed to a human.

The outcome

After deployment, the AI assistant handled around 80% of incoming questions. The team estimates that this reduces manual support costs by about $2,000 per month, while human agents focus on exceptions and higher-value conversations.

What to validate for your team

  • Test answers against your approved policies before publishing.
  • Measure resolved conversations and human handoffs.
  • Refresh website and FAQ content as policies change.
