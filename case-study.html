E-commerce store: 80% of support handled by AI

100 daily customer questions → AI resolves 80 → saves $2,000/month in labor

The challenge

A mid-size online retailer received roughly 100 customer support questions per day — order status, shipping, returns, and product specs. A team of three agents could not scale without rising headcount costs.

The solution

The merchant deployed Sitp GPT with website training (product pages, policies, FAQs) and embedded the chat widget on checkout and account pages. WhatsApp was connected for post-purchase support.

  • Trained on 100+ catalog and policy pages
  • FAQ auto-generated from shipping & return docs
  • Human escalation for refunds and edge cases
  • Email capture on high-intent product questions

Results

Within six weeks, 80% of inbound questions were answered without human intervention. The team focused on complex cases and proactive outreach. Estimated labor savings: $2,000 per month (roughly 1.5 FTE deflected).
