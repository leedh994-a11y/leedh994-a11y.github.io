The challenge

Before Sitp GPT, this e-commerce client's support team answered around 100 customer questions every single day — order status, shipping times, returns, sizing, payment issues. The same questions came in over and over, agents worked overtime, and customers still waited hours for a reply outside business hours.

The solution

The store deployed the Sitp GPT AI customer support bot with the one-time $599 installation service:

  • Trained the AI on the store's product catalog, shipping policy, returns policy and past FAQ — using website pages and PDF documents.
  • Installed the website bot on every page of the storefront with a single embed snippet.
  • Set up FAQ quick prompts so shoppers get one-tap answers to the most common questions.
  • Optimized the support workflow: AI answers first, and only complex cases escalate to a human agent.
  • Connected WhatsApp and Slack so the team gets escalations and daily email summaries where they already work.

The results

Within the first month:

  • ✅ AI now handles 80% of all support questions — about 80 of the 100 daily tickets never reach a human.
  • ✅ $2,000/month in labor costs saved — the team reassigned support hours to growth work.
  • ✅ Average first-response time dropped from hours to under 3 seconds, 24/7 and in 95+ languages.
  • ✅ The bot captures customer emails during chats, feeding the store's marketing list automatically.
  • ✅ Weekly enterprise analytics reports show exactly what customers ask about most — driving product page improvements.
