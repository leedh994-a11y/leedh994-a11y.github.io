The challenge

The store's small team was overwhelmed. Roughly 100 questions came in every day — order status, returns, shipping times, product details — and most of them were repetitive. First responses often took hours, especially outside business hours, and the team was spending most of its day copy-pasting the same answers.

The solution

They trained Sitp GPT on their existing website pages, product FAQs, and shipping/returns policy documents. Within minutes, the AI assistant could answer the most common questions accurately. It was embedded on the storefront and connected to WhatsApp, with automatic escalation to a human whenever a shopper needed extra help.

  • Trained on website content, PDFs, and FAQs
  • Embedded on the site and WhatsApp
  • Auto-updates answers when the website changes
  • Captures shopper emails for follow-up
  • Escalates complex cases to a human agent

The results

Within the first month, AI was resolving 80% of the ~100 daily questions with no human involvement. First-response time dropped from hours to seconds, and the team reclaimed the equivalent of a part-time support role — about $2,000/month in labor costs. Enterprise analytics reports showed exactly which questions came up most, so the team improved product pages to prevent them entirely.

"It's like hiring a first-line support agent that never sleeps. Our team now only touches the 20% of tickets that actually need a human." — Store owner
