Using Sitp GPT as an AI support employee

Onboarding your AI agent

  1. Define scope: what should AI answer vs. escalate?
  2. Feed knowledge: site crawl, PDFs, FAQs, macros
  3. Set tone and quick prompts for common intents
  4. Test with 10 free AI support sessions

Daily operations

  • Review daily email summaries of conversations
  • Update training when products or policies change (auto on Pro)
  • Use analytics to find missing FAQ topics
  • Route VIP and angry customers to humans fast

Quality & compliance
