How to work with your AI employee, day to day

Think of Sitp GPT as a new team member on first-line support. Here's how to onboard it, manage it, and get the most out of it.

Day 1 — Onboarding your AI employee

  • Give it the docs a new hire would get: policies, product pages, price lists (see the setup tutorial).
  • Test it with your 10 free AI support test replies before putting it in front of customers.
  • Set its boundaries: which topics it answers, which always escalate to a human.

Daily routine (5 minutes)

  • 📧 Read the daily email summary: number of conversations, AI resolution rate, escalations.
  • 💬 Check your Slack channel for the escalations queue — each comes with full conversation context.
  • 📥 Export newly captured customer emails to your CRM or mailing list.

Weekly routine (30 minutes)

  • 📊 Review the enterprise analytics report: top question topics reveal what your website should explain better.
  • 📚 Patch knowledge gaps: if the AI answered something poorly, add or correct training content — auto-sync handles website changes automatically.
  • 🎯 Update quick prompts to match seasonal questions (sales, holidays, new products).
