Automate your customer support with AI — full tutorial

Follow these steps to go from zero to a fully automated AI support system. Total setup time: about 30 minutes (or let us do it with the $599 setup service).

Step 1 — Create your knowledge base

Add training content in any of these ways (Free plan: 1 knowledge base, up to 100 website pages):

  • Paste your website URL — Sitp GPT crawls and learns your pages automatically.
  • Upload PDFs, Word docs, or Markdown files (product manuals, policies, price lists).
  • Paste raw text — FAQs, canned answers, policy snippets.

Step 2 — Test the AI answers

Use your 10 free AI support test replies to ask the questions your customers ask most. Check accuracy, then refine your training content where needed.

Step 3 — Install the chatbot on your website

Copy the embed snippet and paste it before </body> on your site. Works with Shopify, WordPress, Wix, Webflow, and custom sites:

<script src="https://yoursite.asia/assets/powered-by.js"></script>
<script src="https://yoursite.asia/widget.js" data-bot-id="YOUR_BOT_ID"></script>

Step 4 — Automate FAQs with quick prompts

Add one-tap quick prompts ("Where is my order?", "What's your return policy?") so customers get instant answers without typing. Generate a starter set with the AI FAQ Generator.

Step 5 — Connect WhatsApp and Slack (paid plans)

Route customer chats from WhatsApp to your AI, and send human-escalations plus daily summaries into Slack. Included in every paid plan.

Step 6 — Turn on auto-sync and analytics (paid plans)

  • Auto-sync: the AI re-trains automatically whenever your website content changes.
  • Email capture: collect customer emails during chats and export them to your CRM.
  • Enterprise analytics reports: weekly reports on top questions, AI resolution rate and captured leads.

Step 7 — Set escalation rules

Decide which topics always go to a human (refund disputes, account security). The AI hands over the full conversation context — see the AI employee guide for daily-operations tips.
