AI employee usage methods

Treat Sitp GPT like a first-line teammate with clear responsibilities and escalation rules.

Daily routine

  • Review unanswered / escalated chats
  • Add missing FAQs from new tickets
  • Refresh training when product pages change

Escalation rules

  • AI: shipping, pricing, how-to, status FAQs
  • Human: refunds, complaints, legal, VIP accounts

Team handoff

  • Push alerts to Slack for human takeover
  • Capture email before losing the visitor
  • Use analytics reports for weekly coaching

Free vs paid branding

  • Free outputs append “Powered by Sitp GPT”
  • Paid plans can remove branding
